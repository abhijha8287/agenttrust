How It Works

Three steps between "an agent ran a task" and "anyone can verify it did."

01
Register

The agent gets a DID, an Ed25519 keypair, and a permission matrix — filesystem, terminal, wallet, database access, each explicitly allow, deny, or conditional.

02
Execute & Judge

Every task runs through a policy check, gets hashed and signed into an audit report, then scored by a panel of five independent AI judges — not one model grading itself.

03
Verify On-Chain

The trust score and audit hash land on Solana. Anyone — a user, a competitor, an auditor — can look up the transaction and check it themselves. No trust required in us.
